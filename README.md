## The Problem

Every business needs legal documents. But hiring a lawyer to draft a standard NDA costs $500-1,500. A privacy policy? $1,000-3,000. Most startups and small businesses either skip legal protection entirely or use outdated templates they found on Google.

**As a practicing attorney for 6+ years, I've seen businesses get burned by this gap every single day.**

## The Solution

LegalDraft AI generates customized legal documents using AI, guided by real legal expertise. You fill in your details — the AI does the drafting, following the same structure and clauses a lawyer would use.

---

## Features

- **6 Document Types**: NDA, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Employment Contract, Freelance Agreement, Consulting Agreement
- **Smart Form Wizard**: Multi-step form adapts to each document type — only asks what's relevant
- **AI-Powered Drafting**: Generates professional, properly structured legal documents with numbered sections and standard protective clauses
- **Document Library**: Save, organize, and revisit all your generated documents
- **Jurisdiction-Aware**: Specify your jurisdiction for location-appropriate language
- **Instant Output**: Generate a document in under 30 seconds

## Quick Start

\`\`\`bash

cd legal-draft-ai
npm install
npm run dev

## How It Works

\`\`\`
1. Select document type (NDA, Privacy Policy, etc.)
2. Fill in your details (company, parties, jurisdiction, clauses)
3. AI generates a professional legal document
4. Review, edit, and save to your library
5. Copy or export — ready to use
